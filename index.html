UCC Math Society Tournament

2020-2021

Details

All details on this page are subject to change and to be confirmed.

The Five Rounds
- The Geometry Round at the end of October
- The Number Theory Round in early December
- The Algebra Round at the end of January
- The Counting and Probability at the end of February
- The Final Round at the end of April

Each Round
- Each round will consist of 8 problems over 45 minutes ranging in difficulty. Each problem will be worth 1-2 marks.
- Each round will be hosted using an online platform. Details to follow.
- Questions will be approachable to students of all grades.

Results
- Participants will receive their results and ranking (overall and within their grade) after each round.
- Final scores and overall winners will be calculated and determined by each participant's three best placements (so missing or making mistakes on one round will not have a big impact).

Prizes and Recognition
- Overall winner takes home the UCC Math Society Cup trophy.
- Top scorers will split a cash prize pool.
- Winners in each grade will also receive prize certificates.
- Prize distribution ceremony will be held during an assembly.
- Top scores for each round will also be published.

Tournament Committee
- Mr. Damion Walker, Faculty Advisor of the UCC Math Society
- Kevin Liu, Head of UCC Math Society
- TBD, Co-head of UCC Math Society

Preparation
- The Math Society will discuss problems similar to those in each competition round during its regular club meetings, so please join the club!
